Who We Are

Serving Traditional South Indian Hospitality

At Thulir Catering Service, we carry forward the rich heritage of South Indian culinary traditions. We believe food is not just nourishment, but a celebration of culture, flavor, and warm hospitality.

Hygienic Cooking

Strict sanitation protocols, masks, hairnets, and sterilized stainless steel utensils in every prep stage.

Premium Ingredients

Sourced locally, freshly ground home-style spice mixes, and top-tier quality grains for authentic tastes.

Experienced Chefs

Masters of traditional arts from across South India, delivering taste consistently and flawlessly.
